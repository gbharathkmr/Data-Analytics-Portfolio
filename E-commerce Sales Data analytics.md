# Australian E-Commerce Sales & Commercial Analysis
**A Data-Driven Study of Seasonality, Product Performance, and Revenue Optimization**

## 📊 Commercial Executive Summary
This project analyzes a full year of sales data for an Australian-based e-commerce retailer, identifying key growth drivers and providing actionable commercial recommendations.

### 1. Key Performance Dashboard
We categorized annual performance, city-level distribution, and price sensitivity.
![E-Commerce Performance Dashboard](ecom_sales_dashboard.png)

#### High-Impact Findings:
* **Total Annual Revenue:** $10.5M
* **Peak Seasonality:** Sales spike in **Month 8 (August)**, aligning with end-of-winter stock clearance.
* **Top City:** Sydney generated the highest revenue share.
* **Operational Opportunity:** "Price Sensitivity" analysis shows opportunities for volume-based discounting.

### 2. Product Revenue Analysis
Analyzed revenue contribution by SKU to prioritize stock management and marketing spend.
![Top Revenue Products](ecom_revenue_by_product.png)

#### Key Insight:
* **Revenue Drivers:** Premium electronics (e.g., MacBook Pro Laptop) generate the highest dollar value, but essential accessories (e.g., USB-C Charging Cable) drive high transaction volume, which represents a prime **upselling opportunity**.
